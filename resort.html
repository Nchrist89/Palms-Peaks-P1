Welcome to Palms and Peaks

At the foot of a majestic dormant volcano, Palms and Peaks is a breathtaking tropical resort where lush landscapes meet thrilling adventures. Originally established as a wildlife park dedicated to preserving the islands unique fauna, our sanctuary has evolved into a premier getaway for those seeking relaxation, excitement, and the stunning beauty of nature.

At Palms and Peaks, you'll find a captivating blend of experiences. Wake up to the gentle sounds of wildlife, explore vibrant beaches, and bask in the warm tropical sun. Whether you're hiking the scenic trails that wind through our expansive grounds or enjoying serene moments at our nature-watching spots, there's something here for everyone.

But the adventure doesn't stop there! We're thrilled to announce our expansion, which will soon include exciting roller coasters and exhilarating attractions, adding a new layer of fun to your stay. Get ready to embark on unforgettable sightseeing tours, engage in vibrant camping experiences, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty that surrounds us.

Join us at Palms and Peaks where the tranquility of nature meets the thrill of adventure. Your dream getaway awaits!
