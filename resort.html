Palms and Peaks

At the foot of a majestic dormant volcano, Palms and Peaks is a breathtaking tropical resort where lush landscapes meet thrilling adventures. Originally established as a wildlife park in the 1980's, dedicated to preserving the islands unique fauna, our sanctuary has evolved into a premier getaway for those seeking relaxation, excitement, and the stunning beauty of nature.

At Palms and Peaks, you'll find a captivating blend of experiences. Wake up to the gentle sounds of wildlife, explore vibrant beaches, and bask in the warm tropical sun. Whether you're hiking the scenic trails that wind through our expansive grounds or enjoying serene moments at our nature-watching spots, there's something here for everyone.

But the adventure doesn't stop there! in the past year due to demand in visitors we are thrilled to announce our expansion, which will soon include exciting roller coasters and exhilarating attractions, adding a new layer of fun to your stay. Get ready to embark on unforgettable sightseeing tours, engage in vibrant camping experiences, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty that surrounds us.

Join us at Palms and Peaks where the tranquility of nature meets the thrill of adventure. Your dream getaway awaits!

Animals

At the resort, visitors can immerse themselves in a vibrant ecosystem brimming with diverse wildlife. Among the highlights are a multitude of tropical birds, like colorful toucans, playful parrots, and dazzling hummingbirds, each bursting with unique plumage and personality. These feathered friends often flutter around the resort, creating a lively atmosphere.

The aquatic life is equally enchanting, featuring an array of rare fish species that inhabit the resort's pristine waters. Guests can expect to see everything from the striking clownfish to majestic angelfish, all displaying remarkable colors and patterns.

For those seeking a more interactive experience, the resort offers opportunities to hand-feed certain animals, making for unforgettable encounters. Adventurous visitors may even have the chance to hold some of the friendlier creatures, like gentle reptiles or small mammals, if they're feeling brave.

Throughout the resort, informative displays provide rich insights into these animals diets, habitats, and behaviors. Guests can learn about what each species eats, their unique traits, and conservation efforts underway to protect them. This educational aspect enhances the experience, fostering a deeper appreciation for the astonishing wildlife that thrives in this tropical paradise.

Take a look at the photo gallary to take a look at some of the animals you can see at the resort!

Exploration

The resort offers an exciting range of sightseeing opportunities, ensuring that every visitor finds something that piques their interest. Various guided tours cater to different preferences, whether you're keen on exploring lush jungles, discovering hidden waterfalls, or observing wildlife in their natural habitats. Each tour is tailored to provide unique experiences, allowing guests to choose based on what they wish to see.

For those who prefer to navigate on their own, detailed maps are available for purchase at one of the resort's shops, along with hiking gear for those looking to venture into the great outdoors. These resources make it easy for guests to explore the stunning landscapes at their own pace.

Families will appreciate the special kids tours designed just for younger adventurers, featuring interactive activities and engaging storytelling that make learning about nature fun. Additionally, the resort hosts themed events throughout the year, with lively parties that delight children and create lasting memories. These festive occasions offer entertainment, games, and opportunities for kids to connect with nature and each other, ensuring that every family member has an enjoyable experience.
