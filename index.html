About Us!

Majestic

At the foot of a majestic dormant volcano lies Palms and Peaks, a stunning tropical resort where lush landscapes blend with thrilling adventures. Established in the 1980s as a wildlife sanctuary dedicated to preserving the island's unique fauna, it has evolved into a premier getaway for relaxation and excitement.

Captivating

At Palms and Peaks, a captivating array of experiences awaits. Wake up to the gentle sounds of wildlife, explore vibrant beaches, and bask in the warm tropical sun. Enjoy moments at our nature-watching spots, and discover activities that cater to all ages, ensuring there's something special for every visitor.

Thrilling

The adventure doesn't end here! In response to increased visitor demand, we are excited to announce our expansion plans. Soon, you'll enjoy thrilling roller coasters and exhilarating attractions that add a new dimension of fun to your stay. Prepare for unforgettable sightseeing tours and engaging camping experiences.

Nature

Immerse yourself in the natural beauty surrounding us at Palms and Peaks and where the dream started. Join us for a dream getaway that combines relaxation with excitement and exploration. Your perfect escape into nature awaits, filled with unforgettable memories and experiences designed just for you!
