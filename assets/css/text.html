A Majestic Getaway

At the foot of a majestic dormant volcano lies Palms and Peaks, a stunning tropical resort where lush landscapes blend with thrilling adventures. Established in the 1980s as a wildlife sanctuary dedicated to preserving the island's unique fauna, it has evolved into a premier getaway for relaxation and excitement.

A Captivating Experience

At Palms and Peaks, a captivating array of experiences awaits. Wake up to the gentle sounds of wildlife, explore vibrant beaches, and bask in the warm tropical sun. Enjoy moments at our nature-watching spots, and discover activities that cater to all ages, ensuring there's something special for every visitor.

ANIMALS

vibrant

At the resort, visitors are welcomed by a vibrant ecosystem teeming with diverse wildlife. Tropical birds, such as colorful toucans and playful parrots, grace the skies with their stunning plumage. Their lively presence fills the air with joy, inviting guests to experience nature's beauty right at their doorstep.

Wonders

The resort's pristine waters host a magnificent array of aquatic life. Guests can marvel at rare fish species, including striking clownfish and majestic angelfish, all showcasing brilliant colors and unique patterns. The chance to interact with these mesmerizing creatures creates unforgettable memories, enhancing the overall experience for all visitors.

Nature

Throughout the resort, informative displays offer insights into wildlife diets, habitats, and behaviors. Guests learn about each species' unique traits and the ongoing conservation efforts to protect them. This educational experience cultivates a deeper appreciation for the incredible flora and fauna that thrive in this tropical paradise.

Experience

Don't miss the chance to explore the photo gallery, showcasing some of the incredible animals found at the resort! There's so much more to discover, and each visit reveals new wonders. Plan your stay now and immerse yourself in the enchanting wildlife that this paradise has to offer!

Tours

These engaging tours foster curiosity and exploration, allowing children to connect with the environment while having fun. Every child leaves with treasured memories of their adventures.

Events

In addition to tours, the resort hosts themed events throughout the year, providing lively entertainment for all ages. These events ensure every family member enjoys an enriching and memorable experience during their stay.
